AgentTown is a chat-driven workspace for AI-native teams.

Turn plain chat messages into runnable mini apps, then execute and write back summaries to keep everyone aligned.

Core capabilities:
- Mini app generation from natural language prompts.
- Editable fields and validation before running.
- Runtime execution with summary writeback to chat.
- Team collaboration with bots, tasks, and grouped conversations.
- World-map style interface for navigating team contexts.
- Bilingual UI: English and Chinese.

Use AgentTown to move from discussion to execution faster.
